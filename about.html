About me

Hey, I'm Alfred —
a designer who builds.

I'm a Senior Product Designer based in Toronto with over eight years of experience designing for FinTech and consumer mobile. I combine research, systems thinking, and front-end literacy to produce work that's both considered and shippable.

Before product design, I trained as an architect — which means I think in systems, take constraints seriously, and understand that the best designs are the ones that hold up when you zoom out.
